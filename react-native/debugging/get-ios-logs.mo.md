# [MO] Get iOS Logs

## Owner: [Julien Nassar](https://github.com/juliennassar)

### Prerequisites

- [ ] Have Xcode installed

## Steps

- Open **Xcode**
- Click on *window > Devices and simulators*
- Select your device and open logs in the window
